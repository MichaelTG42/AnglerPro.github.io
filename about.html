About Us

Angler Pro started as a single one-stop-shop for the local and tourist fishermen along the Lake Michigan border. We have now grown into a small chain of five physical store fronts located along the Lake Michigan shore line as well as luanching an online store. Our products are of the highest quality. We sell all the top-rated fishing bait and lures available on the market. Our staff members can assist you with all your questions from rigging techniques to the bait or lure best suited for your desired catch. We hope to see you at one of our five locations, or that you enjoy shopping over our website.
