Senko

Product Description

If there's been one lure that has changed the face of fishing over the years, it's the Senko. With its wide range of uses and subtle natural action, the Senko has quickly become a favorite with anglers all over the world. The beauty of the Senko is in it's simplicity. The Senko may just look like a thick round plastic worm but the fall rate created from the large amount of salt impregnated in the body drives fish mad. When rigged weightless the Senko falls horizontally with a seductive side to side tail action. Try wacky rigging or texas rigging the Senko and experience the versatility and effectiveness it provides.
